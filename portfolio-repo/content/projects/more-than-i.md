A grassroots initiative centered around community and social work, grounded in psychological care and principles. Designed a logo mark and curated typefaces — pairing the Filipino sans serif workhorse Bantayog Sans with the legible, accessible Helvetica Neue — to express the community-centered nature of the organization. Translated organizational values like empathy, approachability, and professional integrity into a brand system that disarms the calculated demeanor of traditional psychology, applied across delivery vehicles, packaging, ID badges, and relief package collateral.
