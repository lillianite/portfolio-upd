Bright, loud, and grunge-adjacent messaging built for the alternative scene and bold enough for flexible, direct communication. Designed a custom wordmark and logo system using a hand-built "Hideous" display face alongside Work Sans, applied across posters, zines, screening visuals, and on-the-ground messaging for the transgender arts community. Directed a full-scale screening event reaching 190+ audience members across digital and live channels, managing every visual touchpoint from concept to final published output.
