Balanced literary esteem with accessible visual language and evolution for the 25th silver anniversary. The 24th Iyas (2025) paired EB Garamond with Gotham; the 25th Iyas (2026) shifted to ITC Zapf Chancery and Biennale, marking growth while preserving institutional legacy. Built a modular visual identity — covering certificates, ID badges, posters, tote bags, and event programs — designed for iterational change across cohorts without diminishing institutional history. Generated a 19,600% increase in interactions and 235.5% sustained engagement growth across two campaign windows.
