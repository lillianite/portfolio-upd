Themed "Mahika: Gawa Mula sa Wala" (Magic Made From Nothing), drawing on Filipino mythology and avant-garde history with a sharp contrast palette and modern Filipiniana styling — referencing the work of Filipino designers Happy Andrada, Barge Ramos, and Patis Tesoro. Paired Maragsa and Apple Garamond with the textile-inspired Tan Harmoni, and incorporated baybayin script across the brand mark to honor ancestral techniques of communication. Developed the full creative concept and built a 5-month production timeline, leading cross-functional teams in marketing, production design, and event planning, and reaching 200+ communication students and media professionals.
